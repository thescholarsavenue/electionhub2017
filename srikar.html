Proposals

1. I ​propose to initiate a Humor Club​ for the students of IIT Kharagpur under the ambit of the Gymkhana

Comedy as an art form has been gaining massive popularity over the years, and we have a sizable portion of the institute populace who would like to engage in this art form. Humor is an exciting new topic for the students to explore, and we have many enthusiastic and talented students willing to pursue it.

Humor Club is an organization which shall be under the aegis of the Technology Students’ Gymkhana. The Club shall run by its members who shall be conducting Workshops and open mic nights. The Humor Club shall provide an opportunity to all students of the campus to showcase the efforts made by them in ever growing performing art of comedy.

Conceptualization

The reason behind the conceptualization of the Humor club is the opinion of general kgp populace. The introduction of Gymkhana activities in the genre of comedy was a common suggestion during campaigning as a candidate in various hall days. The Humor Club shall serve this purpose.

Regarding this proposal,a comprehensive sample survey was conducted among 467 students across all years and all hall of residence and found out that while 66% of the people were supporting the introduction, 11% actually wanted to be active member of Humor Club and regularly take part in events and sessions to be conducted in such a Club. The survey clearly shows the eagerness of the students at IIT Kharagpur to participate in humor related activities.

Moreover in the academic session 2015­16 a group of enthusiastic students had organised two open mic standup comedy nights like “Live at the Apollo 13” which showed great amount of participation as well as crowd appreciation. We have in touch with the organisers of the open mic night and they have agreed to take this initiative forward along side us.

Benefits of the Humor Club

Providing the students of IIT Kharagpur a chance to showcase and hone their talent in the art of standup comedy.

The growing art of comedy shall be finally getting some Gymkhana activities dedicated to it.

This shall helps students to improve their public speaking and communication skills as well as their confidence.

A successful Humor Club shall ensure the open mic nights becoming landmark events in student life at IIT Kharagpur and shall generally have positive outcome on student community.

2.I​ propose to organise a Freshers’ Cultural Weekend with the aim of introducing to the first year students , the various social and cultural activities of technology students’ gymkhana and making them aware of competitions like open IIT and general championship​.

Events

The events comprise of four genres similar to the general championship namely creative writing ​ elocution​ and ​under literary genre , ​solo vocals ​ solo instruments and ​under entertainment genre, ​solo dance​ dramatic monologue​ and ​ under dramatics genre and sketching under fine arts genre.

Implementation

The programme would commence on Friday night at 8 pm with the sketching event taking place that day. On saturday Creative writing event would take place at 2 pm followed by Solo Instrumentals event and would continue till six thirty in the evening. Following a one hour break the Solo Vocals event would commence at 7:30 pm setting up a fine musical evening. The Dramatic Monologue event would begin early at 10 in the morning on Sunday followed by the elocution event. Finally the Solo Dance event would take place in the evening beginning at 6 pm.

The judging would be done by the Governors of the concerned societies for each event. Judging for the creative writing event would be done by the governors of the Technology Literary Society, while for sketching it would be done by the governors of Spectra. The instrumentals and the vocals events would be judged by the governors of WTMS and ETMS. The governors of ETDS, HTDS, BTDS and TTDS would judge the dramatic monologue event while the governors of the Technology Dance Society would be judging the solo dance event. The governors of their respective societies have been contacted for the same.

Under the supervision of the two Gymkhana General Secretaries the programme would be managed by the team of the Gymkhana Social and Cultural office bearers namely the Literary, Fine and Allied Arts, Entertainment and the Dramatics secretaries. The secretaries would manage their respective events and would report to the General secretaries.

The publicity of the event would be done through email, social media and also by personally addressing the wings in the fresher halls. An email regarding the event would be sent through the Vice President, Technology Students’ Gymkhana to all the freshers. Social Media publicity will also be done by the Gymkhana office bearers. Apart from this the Gymkhana office bearers would be addressing individual wings in the fresher halls in order to ensure hundred percent awareness about the event among the freshers.

Impact

This initiative would provide freshers with a platform at the Gymkhana level to experiment with their talents. It is different from the Open IIT and the Inter Hall events because of its exclusivity for freshers and all the events taking place together during a short period of time which would ensure efficient management. Freshers who are interested in these fields can try out for the events and get hands on experience of the Gymkhana level Social and Cultural activities which would better prepare them for the GC events. Many of the first years are unable to participate in the General championship either due to lack of awareness or they do not completely know their skills. So this fresher’s weekend will be a perfect start for them to discover their prowess and hone their skills further.
