Silla Spandana

Candidate for General Secretary Sports and Games, TSG

SN/IG Hall of Residence

Mechanical Engineering (Manufacturing)


Proposals

1. I propose to provide the refreshments for inter-IIT players in the form of coupons that can be reimbursed at a canteen stocked with nutritionally appropriate food.

The aim of the proposal is: To enable inter­IIT players to choose a diet according to their individual nutritional needs.

Workflow

This proposal will be implemented in the following way:

1. In order to come up with a balanced diet plan, inter­IIT captains, PTIs, coaches and senior players will be contacted.

2. It will be optimised for the Gymkhana budget, which is currently ₹52 per day per person.

3. The diet plan will be approved by a nutritionist selected by the Hall Management Committee.

4.The Technology Students’ Gymkhana will announce a detailed quotation to canteens with the approval of the President, Technology Students’ Gymkhana.

5. A contract will be awarded based on the cost per specified item and a quality check.

6.An approval from the hall warden will be obtained for the canteen to be able to sell the specified products.

7. Inter­IIT players will be given coupons, which can then be reimbursed at the canteen.

Benefits

This proposal will be very beneficial to the student community because:

1. Vegetarians would be able to choose a suitable replacement, such as protein shakes, sprouts, etc instead of egg.

2. There will be a wider choice of food and nutritional supplements.

3. Inter­IIT players will be able to have their dinner at the canteen itself in case they are late for dinner in their respective mess.

4. Food and nutrition is an important aspect of health and fitness, and this initiative will greatly improve these aspects.

2. Addition of digital counterparts of existing events, and the inclusion of a new event in the Cup.

The aim of this proposal is: To create a fun weekend for sports and games along the lines of freshers orientation program held by social and cultural societies.

Features

The proposed sports carnival will have the following features:

1. It will be a compact weekend event encompassing various sports and games.

2. The event will be open to all postgraduate students, research scholars and undergraduate first year students.

3. Students register in teams of three and take part in various events.

4. The sports involved are tennis, football, archery, rollerblading, athletics and wall climbing.

5. Each event will be a short task, the completion of which gives standard po

ints. 6. In tennis, two teams are pitted against each other for a set time limit. The number of points won by each team is the standard points won in this event.

7. In football, two teams play against each other for a fixed time limit of five minutes. The number of goals scored per team is the standard points won by the respective teams in this event.

8. In archery, five tries are given per team. The total points scored is the standard points won in this event.

9. In athletics, standard points will be assigned based on the time taken by the team contestants in a 100m dash.

10. In rollerblading and wall climbing, no standard points will be assigned as such, but goodies will be distributed to the teams who successfully complete the task assigned.

Benefits

This proposal will greatly benefit the general population on campus in these ways:

1. This will encourage bonding of freshers over sports.

2. It will promote a culture of recreational sports.

3. It will encourage a personal one­to­one interaction with senior players and inter­IIT captains.

4. Sports are found to be quite popular will be included in the open IIT sports tournaments

5. This will integrate more postgraduate students and research scholars into the sports community.

3. To organize workshops for interested newbies with no prior knowledge of sports, primarily targeted towards freshers, postgraduate students and research scholars

The aim of this proposal is: To conduct a series of mentoring workshops for initiate newbies into a sport of their choice.

Timeline

The timeline for the execution of this proposal will be as follows:

1. Senior players, inter­IIT players, PTIs and captains will be consulted at the start of the semester.
