Aditya Murthy

Candidate for General Secretary Sports and Games, TSG

Azad Hall of Residence

Chemical Engineering


Proposals

1. Introduction of SPORTS FIESTA, a freshers’ tournament that would be conducted over an extended period of about 4-5 days and strengthening the concept of the Women’s Week

Aim of the program:

- To increase awareness and participation of the UG as well as PG freshers (1st years) at the Inter IIT front

- To capitalize on the talent pool of the first years more efficiently

- To strive harder for the overall awareness and upliftment of sports among women

The present scene

- Every year a very small fraction of 1st year students make it to the Inter IIT Contingent (less than 10% of total contingent size). The main reason for this is accredited to improper channelization and initiation of these budding sportspersons towards the sporting culture of IIT KGP.

- Every year, the Inter Hall tournaments witness brilliant showcase of talent from a number of first year students; proving that talent exists among the first years.

- IIT KGP’s performance could definitely show a marked improvement if proper scouting is done in the initial stages.

- The Women’s Week has shown great potential, though it is yet to achieve its desired heights. Much needs to

Workflow

- Registration links would be provided online for the freshers’ tournament. A good estimate of the number of students willing to turn up will definitely help in organizing the tournament effectively and efficiently.

- The senior players from Lal Bahadur Shastri Hall of Residence shall assist the Gymkhana secretaries to provide for the smooth flow of information regarding the freshers’ tournament. To cater to the post graduate students, offline as well as online publicity would be done.

- Eminent PG players from each sport shall be contacted to help in increasing participation from the PG halls.

- Every effort shall be made to conduct separate tournaments for the Post Graduate students (considering the number of students turning up), the decision for which would be taken by the PTI concerned.

- The tournament shall be supervised by the concerned PTIs along with the senior Inter IIT players.

- The tournament shall simultaneously be accompanied with exercise and drills-sessions, lasting for about 4-5 days, which should be ample for any budding sportsperson to showcase his/her talent.

- The exact schedule shall be decided by the PTIs and the ex-captains/senior players of each sport respectively.

- The concept of Women’s week has provided a start to the upliftment of sports among women. This year it shall be conducted with increased preparations and publicity for each event. The meritorious performers of last year’s edition shall be contacted and we expect an increased participation in each of the events.

The Present Scene

- Injuries among players are more prominent during the winter camp because of the increased frequency and

3.Conduction of a SWIMMING SURVIVAL CAMP to equip the students with basic swimming and survival skills, throughout the academic year 2016-2017.

Aim of the program:

- To equip the students with basic swimming skills to help them in emergency situations.

- To promote the sport of swimming among the students.

- Targets the overall well being of the IIT KGP student community

The Present Scene:

- There have been recent incidents in the past that have thrown light on how the basic knowledge of swimming could have prevented those mishaps.

- Swimming is a basic survival skill which will always find implication at some point of time.

- There has been a lack of awareness among the students regarding the importance of swimming.

- At present, no such camp is being organized within the institute, which makes it quite necessary to take a step towards its initiation.

Benefits:

- The biggest positive to be extracted from this camp is that students would learn to survive in emergent situations, leading to drastically reduced damage to life.

- Increased awareness and the ability to tackle such situations will altogether drive us to be a smart and safe KGP community.
