Proposals

1. INTRODUCING SOCIAL CUP IN GENERAL CHAMPIONSHIP

I propose to introduce a new cup in the General Championship, Technology Students’ Gymkhana namely ‘SOCIAL CUP ‘which shall be dedicatedly involving events focusing on present social issues.

The events under the Social Cup intend to achieve the goal of providing a dynamic forum of exchange on social issues vital to the under privileged in the areas nearby IIT Kharagpur Campus. Since IIT Kharagpur has always been the home of the brightest minds of the nation, it would be a great idea to collectively encourage students to come forward and propose new visionary ideas on tackling various social issues that are presently prevailing in the society. This shall also serve the purpose to enhance the social awareness among the student body.

The motive of this proposal is to encourage events which directly deal with the social issues, propose feasible solutions and work towards its implementation.

FEATURES:

• Conduction of various events on social front.

• Collaboration with various NGOs and National Service Scheme for implementation of the solutions obtained.

BENEFITS:

• The inclusion of this Cup is a great way to channelize the efforts made by the students towards a social cause.

• Improving the standard of living of the under privileged in and around Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur.

• Enhancing social belongingness among the student community. The details regarding the procedure , events and the people contacted are given in APPENDIX 1

APPENDIX 1:

The events planned to be conducted under the Social Cup are as follows:

1. Social Innovation:

• It will be a social case study event.

• The Problem Statement will be decided in collaboration with the NSS professors and the NGOs.

Timeline:

• The Problem Statement will be released in the month of October.

• Each Hall of Residence will have to submit the most economical solution to the proposed case study by first week of November.

• The results will be declared by the second week of November.

2. Shiksharpan:

Each Hall of Residence will be allotted one school for the academic year

They will be responsible for their educational and social upliftment

Timeline:

The event will be a year long event

The event will be judged at the end of the year

2. PROVIDING NECESSARY EQUIPMENTS FOR ALL THE PARTICIPATING HALLS OF RESIDENCE FOR DRAMATICS EVENTS

I propose to provide a centralised system of equipment that remain common to all the participating Halls of Residence.

It is known that every year during the dramatics events various participating halls arrange for the extra equipment. Hence, I propose to install new equipment for the dramatics events of the General Championship under Technology Students’ Gymkhana as a onetime investment, listed as follows:

1) Babies: 5 are already installed but are of very poor quality

2) Side Spots of greater intensity

3) LED lights / babies different from the normal babies

4) Flicker ( At most 1 )

5) Smoke machine ( at least 2 )

6) Collar mic ( 3 for Netaji Auditorium )

7) Hand mic at least 5

Budget:

1. Babies (Required 6) = Rs. 4500 per piece

2. Spots (Required 4) = Rs. 6800 per piece

3. Flicker Lights (Required 1) = Rs. 4000 per piece

4. Smoke machine (Required 2) = Rs. 5000 per piece

5. Collar Mic (Required 3) = Rs. 3500 per piece

6. Hand Mic (Required 5) = Rs. 2200 per piece

Total – Rs. 89,700 only

Benefits:

1. It is a onetime investment.

2. It is handy.

3. It can be used by other societies as well.

4. Repeated expenditure for every event will be avoided
